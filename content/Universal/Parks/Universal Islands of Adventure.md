# Universal Islands of Adventure

<img src="../../Assets/Park Images/Islands of Adventure/velocicoaster.jpg" alt="Universal Islands of Adventure" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Park role
The most concentrated thrill lineup in the original Universal resort and a major candidate for your strongest Universal day.

## Best use case on this trip
Best if you want a coaster-heavy, high-intensity day with strong Harry Potter payoff and the most obvious headline ride density.

## Current trip fit
- Strong current candidate for **Wed 15 Apr 2026**.
- This is one of the highest-confidence park placements in the draft because it lines up well with **Early Park Admission**, Hagrid's priority logic, and the previously created IOA strategy material.
- If Mariangeles wants a lower-intensity version of this day, the plan should keep Harry Potter payoff while avoiding overcommitting to coaster-heavy routing.

## Early-admission posture
See [[../Strategy/Early Park Admission]]. Often the park where early admission matters most if your top targets include Hagrid's or major coasters.

## Community-sentiment summary
- Strongest public-love cluster: **Hagrid's, VelociCoaster, Spider-Man, Forbidden Journey, Hulk**
- Best conditional-value cluster: **Jurassic Park River Adventure, Ripsaw Falls, Bilge-Rat Barges** depending on weather / soak tolerance
- Lower-value cluster for your trip style: kid play areas and light Seuss fillers unless you want a decompress block

## Dining summary

### Lower-priority food if optimizing quality
- Burger Digs

## Strategy sketch
- Morning: aim at the hardest-to-recover headliner first
- Midday: decide whether to continue thrill stacking or pivot to water rides / lower-intensity zones
- Later day: only hop once the major targets are protected

## Friction points
- Multiple rides here can dominate standby waits
- Water rides are great only if weather and tolerance line up
- It is easy to overstack intensity and burn energy early

## Open questions
- Is Hagrid's the #1 Universal priority?
- How much coaster repetition do you actually want?
- Are you okay getting soaked on water rides if conditions are good?

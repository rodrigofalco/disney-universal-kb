# Disney's Hollywood Studios

<img src="../../Assets/Park Images/Hollywood Studios/hollywood-studios-tower-of-terror.jpg" alt="Disney's Hollywood Studios" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Park role
Most top-heavy Disney park for headliners. Strong candidate for the most aggressive Disney rope drop.

## Best use case on this trip
Best for a high-intensity day built around Star Wars, thrill rides, and a few major attractions that build long waits fast.

## Current trip fit
- Strong current candidate for **Tue 21 Apr 2026**.
- This is another clean **Pop Century + Skyliner** fit and probably one of the highest-value Disney mornings to execute aggressively.
- If EPCOT stays on 20 Apr, this sequence creates a sensible two-day Skyliner block before the later flex/shopping candidate day.

## Early-entry posture
See [[../Strategy/Early Theme Park Entry]]. This is one of the highest-value parks for executing early entry well — use it to get one headliner before standby compounds.

## Lightning Lane posture
See [[../Strategy/Lightning Lane]] for full tier lists and strategy. Summary:
- **Single Pass (buy separately):** Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance — don't skip; standby can hit 80–120 min
- **Multi Pass Tier 1 (max 1 advance):** Slinky Dog Dash, Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway, Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run
- **Multi Pass Tier 2:** The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror, Toy Story Mania!, Star Tours, Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular, Alien Swirling Saucers, Frozen Sing-Along

**Recommended LLMP pick:** Tier 1 = Slinky Dog Dash or Runaway Railway; Tier 2 = Tower of Terror + Toy Story Mania!
**Recommended LLSP:** Rise of the Resistance.

## Land layout and attraction clustering

| Land / Area | Key attractions | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Hollywood Blvd & Echo Lake** | Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular, Star Tours, For the First Time in Forever (Frozen Sing-Along) | Entry corridor area; Indy show is Echo Lake area |
| **Sunset Boulevard** | Tower of Terror, ~~Rock 'n' Roller Coaster~~ (closed), Fantasmic! amphitheater | Tower is the dominant ride here; Coaster closed |
| **Toy Story Land** | Slinky Dog Dash, Toy Story Mania!, Alien Swirling Saucers | Compact area, opens early; often very crowded by 10 AM |
| **Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge** | Rise of the Resistance, Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run | Far back of the park; most immersive area |
| **Grand Ave / Animation Courtyard** | Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway | Center of park |

**Routing tip:** The efficient morning loop depends on your LL strategy. If using Rise LLSP + a Tier 1 LLMP: rope drop Toy Story Land (Slinky Dog or Runaway Railway standby), then pivot to Galaxy's Edge when Rise LL window opens. Tower of Terror midday or afternoon via LLMP.

## Strategy sketch
- **Morning / early entry:** Prioritize Toy Story Land (Slinky Dog standby is shortest at opening) or use early entry to reach Galaxy's Edge before it crowds.
- **Midday:** Use LL refreshes for Tower of Terror, Toy Story Mania!. Shows (Indy, Frozen Sing-Along) work well during heat peak.
- **Evening:** Either Fantasmic! or extended Galaxy's Edge time. Decide before the evening because Fantasmic! seating works better with early positioning.

## Friction points
- This park has less filler than Magic Kingdom — bad waits hurt more.
- Headliners are concentrated and all build long queues fast.
- Galaxy's Edge is far from Toy Story Land, making same-morning loops expensive.
- Fantasmic! timing forces an early evening decision.

## Park day sequencing — recommendation

You have 4 parks and 6 nights. This leaves 2 days for resort, Disney Springs, rest, or re-visits.

### Suggested order based on energy and crowd logic

| Day suggestion | Park | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| **Day 1 Disney** | Disney's Hollywood Studios | Best park to rope-drop hard at the start of the Disney block while energy is fresh. Headliners load fast. No major nighttime anchor required if tired. |
| **Day 2 Disney** | Disney's Animal Kingdom | Early morning advantage for safari + Pandora. Easiest park to finish by afternoon — good for mid-trip recovery. |
| **Day 3 Disney** | Magic Kingdom | Full day justified; castle + fireworks payoff. Can absorb a more relaxed pace if earlier days were tiring. |
| **Day 4 Disney** | EPCOT | Flexible start; Flower & Garden Festival adds food/atmosphere payoff. Best park for a later arrival if fatigued. Strong evening close with Luminous. |

**Alternative:** If you want the fireworks experience earlier in the trip (e.g., on day 1 or 2), swap Magic Kingdom earlier. The above order is for efficiency; the right order depends on preferences.

---

## High-level sequencing logic

- **Hollywood Studios** and **Animal Kingdom** reward strong early starts most.
- **Magic Kingdom** justifies a full day because it combines volume + nighttime payoff (fireworks).
- **EPCOT** is the most flexible — can start later and still have a full, valuable day due to the Flower & Garden Festival.

## Critical April 2026 planning notes

- **Big Thunder Mountain Railroad is CLOSED** through early May 2026 — remove from all MK plans.
- **Rock 'n' Roller Coaster is CLOSED** for Muppets retheme — remove from all DHS plans.
- **EPCOT Flower & Garden Festival** runs your entire Disney stay — plan it into the EPCOT day.
- **Dining reservation window is open now** — if ADRs not booked, act immediately. See [[Strategy/Dining Reservations]].
- **Lightning Lane Single Passes** for TRON, Rise of the Resistance, Guardians, and Flight of Passage should be booked 7 days before each park day (Pop Century resort benefit).

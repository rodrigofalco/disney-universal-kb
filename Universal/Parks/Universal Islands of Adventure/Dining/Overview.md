- Where a public score was available from TouringPlans, it is included below
- Menus, hours, mobile order, and table-service operations are variable: **verify closer to trip**

## Practical recommendations for this trip
- **Best bets:** Mythos Restaurant, Three Broomsticks, Green Eggs and Ham Cafe, Croissant Moon Bakery, Thunder Falls Terrace
- **Lower priority if optimizing food quality or time:** Burger Digs, Captain America Diner, Comic Strip Cafe

# Avatar: Flight of Passage

<img src="../../../../../Assets/Attraction Images/Animal Kingdom/avatar-flight-of-passage.jpg" alt="Avatar Flight of Passage" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />


## Type
- Simulator (bike-mount flying simulator with screen and physical motion)

## Location
- **Land:** Pandora – The World of Avatar, Disney's Animal Kingdom
- Far left of the park; the most visited area of Animal Kingdom

## Community sentiment score
- **9.8/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- Guests sit astride a "mountain banshee" simulator bike that pitches, rolls, and responds physically while a large screen projects an immersive flight over Pandora. Scent, mist, and wind effects augment the experience. One of the most highly rated theme park rides in the world.
- Ride duration: ~5 min (feels longer due to immersion)

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 44" (112 cm)
- **Intensity:** Moderate — the physical sensations are strong (pitching forward and leaning into banking turns) but no g-forces or drops. The bike seat presses against the rider's chest, which some guests find claustrophobic.
- **Motion sickness risk:** High for guests sensitive to simulated flight/virtual reality motion. The immersion is deep — more than most Disney simulators.
- **Water:** Light mist effects only
- **Drop:** No physical drop; simulated descent visuals

## Lightning Lane
- **Single Pass only** — not included in Multi Pass
- Approx. price: $15–$19/person (dynamic)
- **Book at 7 AM** (7 days ahead via Pop Century resort benefit) — frequently sells out by 9 AM on busy days
- This is the clearest must-buy Single Pass at Animal Kingdom

## Strategy notes
- **LLSP is essential** — Flight of Passage standby can hit 90–120+ min by mid-morning on moderate crowd days.
- **Morning timing:** Even with LLSP, the Pandora land becomes very crowded early. Consider arriving at Animal Kingdom before park opening and walking toward Pandora immediately.
- **Na'vi River Journey is adjacent** (also in Pandora) — good cluster. Na'vi is Multi Pass, easier to manage alongside FoP via LL refresh.
- **After Pandora:** Cross to Africa for Kilimanjaro Safaris (best when animals are most active in the morning).

## Trip relevance
High — the most-loved attraction in Animal Kingdom and one of the highest-rated rides at Walt Disney World overall. Essential for this park day.

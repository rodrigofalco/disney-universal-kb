# Jurassic World VelociCoaster

## Type
- Coaster (launch coaster; inversions; top-hat element)

## Location
- **Land:** Jurassic World (formerly Jurassic Park), Universal Islands of Adventure
- Near the back of the park; visible by its large top-hat inversion element from much of the park

## Community sentiment score
- **9.7/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- High-intensity launched coaster with multiple inversions, a 155-ft top-hat inversion, and two separate launch sequences. Themed to the InGen raptor training program from Jurassic World. One of the most technically impressive coasters in the US. The queue theming is also strong — don't skip it.
- Ride duration: ~1.5 min (very fast, as high-intensity coasters tend to be)

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 51" (130 cm) — one of the higher height requirements at Universal
- **Intensity:** Very high — multiple inversions, high-speed launches, zero-g roll. One of the most intense coasters in Orlando. Not suitable for guests who don't like inversions or strong g-forces.
- **Motion sickness risk:** High — full inversions and rapid direction changes
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** The top-hat inversion includes a significant height-to-inversion element; not a traditional vertical drop but equally disorienting

## Express Pass
- **Yes — Express Pass covers VelociCoaster**
- Without Express or early arrival: waits regularly hit 45–90 min by mid-morning

## Strategy notes
- **Second morning priority at IOA** (after Hagrid's) — reach VelociCoaster after completing Hagrid's, while waits are still manageable.
- **If you strongly prioritize VelociCoaster over Hagrid's:** Go to VelociCoaster first (it's closer to the IOA entrance via the clockwise route through Jurassic World land) and Hagrid's second. The trade-off: Hagrid's waits grow faster and are harder to recover.
- **Adjacency:** Jurassic World / Jurassic Park area includes Jurassic Park River Adventure (water ride; good afternoon add if weather is right) and Raptor Encounter (character meet). Compact cluster.

## Trip relevance
High for coaster fans — widely considered among the best coasters in the eastern US. If you like intensity, this is a major priority.

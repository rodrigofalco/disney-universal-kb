## ⚠️ Status: Closed — April 2026

**This attraction is closed for a retheme.** It is being refurbished as a Muppets-themed coaster. The expected reopening is **Summer 2026** — it is very unlikely to be operating during your Apr 18–24 Disney stay. Remove it from all plan versions.

- When operating: one of two major coasters at Hollywood Studios (alongside Slinky Dog Dash). 48" height requirement when open.

## Planning implications

- **Do not include in any DHS day plan for Apr 18–24 2026.**
- Tower of Terror (Sunset Boulevard) is still operating and is the dominant Sunset Boulevard attraction during this visit.
- Galaxy's Edge and Toy Story Land carry the park's ride priorities without Rock 'n' Roller Coaster.

## Planning note

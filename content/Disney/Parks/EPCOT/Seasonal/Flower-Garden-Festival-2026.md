# EPCOT International Flower & Garden Festival 2026

## Key facts

- **Dates:** 4 Mar – 1 Jun 2026
- **Your EPCOT visit:** 18–24 Apr 2026 (full overlap — the festival will be active)
- **Cost:** Included with regular EPCOT admission; no extra ticket required
- **Booth hours:** Tue–Thu 11:30 AM – 8:30 PM; Fri–Mon 11:00 AM – 9:00 PM

---

## What it adds to an EPCOT day

- **Outdoor kitchen food booths** throughout the park (~20+ booths, small-plate format ~$5–$10/item)
- **Character and floral topiaries** throughout World Showcase — major photo opportunities
- **Garden Rocks Concert Series** on select evenings (some evenings require a separate ticket)
- Plant-based "Garden Graze" option if interested
- **New for 2026:** Nectar hosted by AdventHealth (new outdoor kitchen); 75+ new menu items across all booths

**Planning implication:** This festival turns EPCOT into a food-stroll park, not just a ride park. Treat 2–3 hours of the afternoon/evening as dedicated booth time if that appeals.

## Strategy for the festival day

### If this is your rides + booths day

- Start with park opening: Guardians LLSP + Remy's or Frozen via LLMP while crowds are light.
- After 2–3 major rides, pivot to World Showcase and start grazing booths when they open at 11:30 AM.
- Route clockwise or counterclockwise around the lagoon — pick a direction and stick to it; doubling back costs 20+ minutes.
- Plan 4–6 booth stops as a "meal" rather than sitting down at a table-service restaurant.
- Fleur de Lys (France) and Hanami (Japan) are perennially well-reviewed; prioritize these.
- End the evening with Luminous – The Symphony of Us at the lagoon if you're staying late.

### If this is your food / atmosphere day (later start acceptable)

- Skip rope drop; arrive by 10 AM.
- Aim for Guardians + Remy's/Frozen LLSP/LLMP in the morning.
- Full afternoon World Showcase booth stroll.
- Finish with Luminous.

### What to skip at the booths

No need to hit every booth. Refreshment Outpost and Refreshment Station are primarily drinks filler. Canada Popcorn Cart is a snack, not a meal stop. Block & Hans is good if craft beer is a priority; skip otherwise.

## Garden Rocks Concert Series

- Concerts typically happen on select evenings at the America Gardens Theatre (near American Adventure pavilion).
- Some require a separate dining package or ticket; others are included with park admission but require a seat reservation.
- **Verify the April schedule** on Disney's official website closer to your visit.
- If Luminous and a Garden Rocks concert fall on the same evening, prioritize based on preference — they overlap in timing.

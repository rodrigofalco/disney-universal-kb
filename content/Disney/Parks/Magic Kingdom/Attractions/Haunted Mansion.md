# Haunted Mansion

<img src="../../../../Assets/Attraction Images/Magic Kingdom/haunted-mansion.jpg" alt="Haunted Mansion" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Type
- Dark ride (slow-moving Doom Buggy vehicle; gothic humor theme)

## Location
- **Land:** Liberty Square, Magic Kingdom
- Between Fantasyland and Frontierland; near Hall of Presidents

## Community sentiment score
- **9.3/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- Classic Disney dark ride through a haunted estate with 999 "Happy Haunting Ghosts." Stretching room entry sequence. Doom Buggy vehicles move through elaborately themed rooms. Mix of humor and mild spooky atmosphere. One of the most beloved and culturally significant rides at Disney World.
- Ride duration: ~9 min (one of the longer Disney dark rides)

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** None
- **Intensity:** Very low — slow, occasionally dark, a few mild jump-scare-style moments. Appropriate for essentially all ages.
- **Motion sickness risk:** Very low
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** There is a short descent (the "stretching room" drops slightly before the ride itself; it's theatrical, not a thrill element)

## Lightning Lane
- **Multi Pass Tier 2** — bookable as part of LLMP 3-ride selection
- Standby can reach 40–60 min on busy days; on moderate late-April days may be manageable via standby during midday lull
- Worth including as a Tier 2 LLMP pick if it fits your plan

## Strategy notes
- **Adjacency:** Liberty Square is a natural pass-through between Fantasyland and Frontierland. Pair with Hall of Presidents (no LL, indoor — good for heat or rain breaks) and Pirates of the Caribbean in Adventureland (Multi Pass Tier 2, a few minutes' walk through the hub).
- Can be used as a midday recovery ride — good air conditioning, long queue capacity means waits absorb quickly when manageable.
- High repeat value if it appeals to you — not a "one and done" ride for fans.

## Trip relevance
High — few Disney rides have Haunted Mansion's broad universal appeal and iconic status. Worth prioritizing for almost any visitor.

## Planning note
Verify status and any seasonal overlays (holiday themed versions typically run only in October/November/December — not expected in April, but verify).

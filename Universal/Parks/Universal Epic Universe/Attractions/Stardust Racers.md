# Stardust Racers

## Type
- Coaster (dual-launch coaster; racing/dueling concept)

## Location
- **World:** Celestial Park (hub), Universal Epic Universe
- Central park hub; visible from multiple worlds as the park's "skyline coaster"

## Community sentiment score
- **9.6/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026 (post-opening). Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- Dual-launch racing coaster where two separate trains launch and race each other through intertwined track. 133 ft tall. The racing/dueling element and the height make this the park's most thrilling coaster. Celestial Park theming (astronomy/cosmos aesthetic) surrounds the structure.
- Ride duration: ~2 min

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 48" (122 cm)
- **Intensity:** High — two separate launch sequences, significant height, racing interaction creates strong speed perception. Comparable to major launch coasters at Universal.
- **Motion sickness risk:** Moderate-high for guests sensitive to speed and lateral movement. No inversions on this ride (unlike VelociCoaster).
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** Height element — the coaster reaches 133 ft; there is a significant drop component

## Express Pass
- **Yes — Express Pass covers Stardust Racers**
- Among the most in-demand rides at Epic Universe

## Strategy notes
- **Second or third priority at Epic Universe** (after Battle at the Ministry and Monsters Unchained, depending on your preferences).
- Best managed via Early Park Admission or Express Pass — the central park location means it is visible and attractive to every guest entering the park.
- **Adjacency:** Located in Celestial Park (the hub connecting all 4 worlds). Good between-world placement — accessible after completing a world without requiring backtracking.
- Constellation Carousel (no height req, family) is adjacent in Celestial Park — useful for a companion who doesn't want to ride Stardust.

## Trip relevance
High for coaster fans — consistently praised as an excellent coaster that anchors the whole park visually and experientially.

## Planning note
Verify operating status and any access rules closer to trip. Epic Universe is new and operational details may evolve.

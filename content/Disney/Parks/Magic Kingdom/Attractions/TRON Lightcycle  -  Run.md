# TRON Lightcycle / Run

## Type
- Coaster (outdoor/indoor launch coaster; motorcycle-position seating)

## Location
- **Land:** Tomorrowland, Magic Kingdom
- Far end of Tomorrowland; the large blue canopy structure is visible from the park entrance

## Community sentiment score
- **9.6/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- High-speed launch coaster where guests straddle a lightcycle vehicle and lean forward. Launches through an outdoor canopy with lighting effects, briefly enters an enclosed building, then back outside. Based on the TRON franchise.
- One of the fastest rides at Walt Disney World. Ride duration is short (~2.5 min).

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 48" (122 cm)
- **Intensity:** High — rapid launch acceleration, high speeds, aggressive lateral movement. The motorcycle lean position is unusual and can feel uncomfortable for guests with upper-body sensitivity.
- **Motion sickness risk:** Moderate-high for guests sensitive to speed and lateral forces. No inversions. No spinning.
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** No significant drops; speed is the primary intensity driver

## Lightning Lane
- **Single Pass only** — not included in Multi Pass
- Approx. price: $19–$23/person (dynamic; verify in MDE app)
- **Pop Century booking day:** 7 days before your MK date. Book at 7 AM.
- Alternative: standby is shortest in the **first 15–30 min of park opening** (or early entry window). Go directly to TRON if using rope drop strategy.

## Strategy notes
- Best approached as either rope-drop-first or Single Pass. Do not assume midday standby will be manageable.
- If using rope drop: walk directly to TRON from the park entrance. Do not stop for photos or food first.
- If buying Single Pass: you can use early entry time for another ride (e.g., Space Mountain or Seven Dwarfs) while TRON Single Pass fills your window.
- **Adjacency:** Space Mountain is close. Do not cluster both at morning opening without LL planning — they compete for the same scarce morning window.

## Trip relevance
High — MK's newest headliner and clearest "must-do for coaster fans" priority.

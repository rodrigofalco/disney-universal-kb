## Why it matters operationally
Pop Century is not just a place to sleep. It affects rope-drop reliability and midday-break viability.

## Important planning advantages
- Eligible for [[../Strategy/Early Theme Park Entry]].
- Disney transportation page indicates Pop Century is connected to the **Disney Skyliner** network for **Hollywood Studios** and **EPCOT** access.
- Disney also indicates **complimentary bus transportation** is available across the resort system, including theme parks and Disney Springs.

## Best practical uses
- Strong base for **Hollywood Studios** and **EPCOT** days because Skyliner access can reduce friction.
- Good option for a midday reset if one of those two park days becomes too hot or too long.
- Less direct strategic advantage for Magic Kingdom than for the Skyliner parks, so that day likely needs an earlier start.

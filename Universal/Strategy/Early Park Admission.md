# Early Park Admission

## What is confirmed
Your Universal booking notes already indicate **Early Park Admission included**. Official Universal language commonly describes this as **up to one hour before regular park opening** at select attractions, with valid theme park admission required.

## What this means for this trip
Since you are staying at [[../Resorts/Universal's Endless Summer Resort - Surfside Inn and Suites]], early admission should be treated as a core advantage, not a bonus.

## Practical strategy
- Be at transportation early enough to be in the arrival wave, not the late-resort wave.
- Use early admission for the single attraction or land that is hardest to recover later.
- Keep your first 2 hours clean: no shopping, minimal photos, food only if it saves later time.
- Because you have park-to-park tickets, design at least one route that uses early admission in one park and hops later for the second half of the day.

## Park-by-park default use
- [[../Parks/Universal Studios Florida]]: strong for Wizarding World access and selected headliners.
- [[../Parks/Universal Islands of Adventure]]: often strongest when aimed at the biggest coaster / Harry Potter demand.
- [[../Parks/Universal Epic Universe]]: likely to be the highest-value early-admission play if eligible attractions line up with your priorities.

## Variable items to verify closer to trip
- Which park gets early admission on each date
- Which rides are participating
- Actual transportation timing from Surfside
- Epic Universe operating pattern once your travel dates get closer

## Operational note
At Universal, being early enough for the **first practical ride cycle** can change the entire day.

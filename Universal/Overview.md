## Park day sequencing — recommendation

| Day suggestion | Park | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| **Day 1 Universal (14 Apr)** | Universal Epic Universe | Newest park; highest crowd demand; use Early Park Admission for maximum advantage. Go here first while energy is highest. |
| **Day 2 Universal (15 Apr)** | Universal Islands of Adventure | Most headliner-dense original park; Hagrid's, VelociCoaster, Spider-Man. Strong second day. |
| **Day 3 Universal (16 Apr)** | Universal Studios Florida | More flexible; Harry Potter + classic rides; better for mixing in hops. Use Park-to-Park on this day to revisit priority parks or Hogwarts Express routing. |

**Hopping on Day 3:** Consider using the Park-to-Park ticket to make a hop on the final Universal day. Hogwarts Express as a cross-park experience works best when you are not in a hurry — it only runs in one direction per station. Easiest hop is USF → IOA in the morning (King's Cross to Hogsmeade) and back.

**Alternative sequencing:** If Epic Universe crowds concern you, some planners prefer doing IOA day 1 (Hagrid's at rope drop is extremely popular) and Epic Universe day 2 or 3. Either approach can work; the key is committing to your top priority and arriving early on that day.

## Key operational facts

- **Endless Summer does NOT include Express Pass** — if you want it, buy it separately. See [[Strategy/Express Pass]].
- **Early Park Admission** (1 hour before park opening) is your primary crowd-beating tool at all three parks.
- **Epic Universe** opened May 2025 and will be ~11 months old during your visit — demand remains elevated, especially for Monsters Unchained and Battle at the Ministry.
- **Hogwarts Express** (Park-to-Park) requires the Park-to-Park ticket you have — use it.

## High-level sequencing logic

- **Epic Universe** is the biggest variable and may deserve the sharpest arrival plan.
- **Islands of Adventure** is the most headliner-dense thrill day.
- **Universal Studios Florida** works well for Harry Potter + classic rides + easier midday hopping.

## April 2026 context

- Your Universal dates (14–17 Apr) are **moderate-crowd** — Easter (5 Apr) has passed by then.
- Epic Universe will still have elevated novelty demand even in moderate periods.
- Mid-April temperatures: ~83°F (28°C); afternoon showers possible. Water rides are practical.

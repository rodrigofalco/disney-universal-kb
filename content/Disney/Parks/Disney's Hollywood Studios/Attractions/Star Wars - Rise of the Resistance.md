# Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance

<img src="../../../../Assets/Attraction Images/Hollywood Studios/star-wars-rise-of-the-resistance.jpg" alt="Star Wars - Rise of the Resistance" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Type
- Dark ride / multi-system attraction (trackless dark ride + walk-through + simulator elements)

## Location
- **Land:** Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, Disney's Hollywood Studios
- Far back of the park, inside Galaxy's Edge; requires walking through the immersive outdoor area to reach it

## Community sentiment score
- **9.8/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- Massive multi-phase attraction combining a pre-show "capture" sequence, a detention cell walkthrough, and a final trackless dark ride through a full-scale Star Destroyer environment. One of the most technically complex theme park attractions in existence. Multiple separate rooms and vehicle sequences.
- Total experience from pre-show to exit: ~18–22 min.

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** None (no height requirement — all ages can ride)
- **Intensity:** Moderate — trackless vehicle movement with sudden direction changes, small drop sensation during one sequence, loud effects. Not a thrill coaster.
- **Motion sickness risk:** Low-moderate — the scale of the effects and some vehicle movements can affect sensitive guests, but it is far less intense than a coaster or simulator
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** One short drop during the hangar sequence (very brief, not steep)

## Lightning Lane
- **Single Pass only** — not included in Multi Pass
- Approx. price: $20–$25/person (highest-priced LLSP at WDW)
- Frequently sells out on busy days; buy at 7 AM (or 7 days ahead via Pop Century resort benefit)
- **Do not skip the Single Pass** — standby during peak hours can reach 80–120+ min

## Strategy notes
- **Single Pass is the right call** for almost every visit. The experience is worth the premium.
- If doing standby: only viable in the first 30–45 min of the park opening or late in the evening.
- **Adjacency:** Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run is steps away in the same Galaxy's Edge area (Multi Pass Tier 1). These two are the most natural cluster in the park but you cannot do both at rope drop on standby — LL one, standby the other, or separate them.
- Galaxy's Edge has strong nighttime atmosphere even after rides are completed — worth returning to in the evening for photos and ambiance.

## Trip relevance
High — the highest-rated attraction at Hollywood Studios and one of the top rides at all of Walt Disney World.

## Planning note
Verify operating status closer to trip; Rise of the Resistance has had periodic technical difficulties that cause unexpected closures.

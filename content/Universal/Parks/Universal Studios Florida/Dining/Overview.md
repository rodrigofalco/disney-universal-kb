## Practical recommendations for this trip
- **Best themed quick-service meal:** Leaky Cauldron
- **Best dessert/snack stop:** Florean Fortescue’s Ice-Cream Parlour
- **Best sit-down break inside the park:** Finnegan's Bar & Grill
- **Best functional coffee/light breakfast stop:** Today Cafe or Starbucks
- **Best avoid-if-optimizing-time-and-food-quality:** lower-rated Springfield quick service unless you specifically want the theming

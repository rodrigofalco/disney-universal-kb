# Harry Potter and the Battle at the Ministry

<img src="../../../../Assets/Attraction Images/Epic Universe/harry-potter-and-the-battle-at-the-ministry.jpg" alt="Harry Potter and the Battle at the Ministry" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />

## Type
- Dark ride (multi-system; elevator-themed entry + trackless dark ride through the Floo Network and Ministry of Magic)

## Location
- **World:** Ministry of Magic, Universal Epic Universe
- Set in a 1920s Paris street exterior that leads into the 1990s Ministry of Magic; accessed through the Floo Network concept

## Community sentiment score
- **9.7/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026 (post-opening). Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- The anchor ride of the Ministry of Magic world. The queue experience begins in a Parisian street, transitions through the Floo Network (fire travel), and deposits guests in the Ministry of Magic where a battle with Death Eaters is underway. The ride technology combines physical sets, screens, and projection mapping at large scale. One of the most ambitious attractions at Epic Universe.
- Based in the Fantastic Beasts era — different from the IOA/USF Harry Potter attractions thematically.

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 40" (102 cm); guests 40"–48" must ride with a companion
- **Intensity:** Moderate — trackless vehicle movement, no inversions, no major drops. Some dark scenes and battle effects may be mildly intense for very young guests.
- **Motion sickness risk:** Low-moderate — less screen-dependent than Gringotts; primarily physical environment movement
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** No drops

## Express Pass
- **Yes — Express Pass covers Battle at the Ministry**
- Without Express or very early arrival: waits can be very long on busy days (Epic Universe novelty demand is elevated throughout 2026)

## Strategy notes
- **Strong candidate for the #1 priority at Epic Universe**, especially for Harry Potter fans. Along with Monsters Unchained, this is the hardest-to-recover high-demand ride at Epic.
- **Best approach:** Use Early Park Admission at Epic Universe. Walk directly to Ministry of Magic and Battle at the Ministry before the regular crowd enters.
- **World atmosphere:** The Ministry of Magic world has rich environmental theming — Parisian streets, wizarding shop fronts. Budget exploration time after completing the ride.
- **Adjacency:** The 1920s Paris Butterbeer is in this world (flavour unique to Ministry of Magic). Ollivanders likely has a presence here — verify.

## Trip relevance
Elite — the defining new Wizarding World attraction and a primary reason Epic Universe is worth a dedicated day.

## Planning note
Epic Universe opened May 2025; operations and access rules may still evolve. Verify queue system (virtual line, timed entry, or standard standby) closer to trip.

# Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts

<img src="../../../../../Assets/Attraction Images/Universal Studios Florida/diagon-alley.jpg" alt="Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />


## Type
- Dark ride / coaster hybrid (multi-screen track ride with coaster elements and physical sets)

## Location
- **Land:** Diagon Alley, Universal Studios Florida
- Inside the Gringotts Bank building at the end of Diagon Alley

## Community sentiment score
- **9.4/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- The anchor attraction in Diagon Alley. Guests board a Gringotts mine cart and travel through bank vaults, encountering dragons, Voldemort, and Bellatrix on screens and physical sets. Combines coaster-like movement with dark ride show scenes and fire/heat effects. The queue through Gringotts Bank is itself elaborate and considered part of the experience.
- Ride duration: ~5 min

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 42" (107 cm)
- **Intensity:** Moderate — coaster movement and some aggressive physical jerks, but no major inversions. Fire blast effect (from the dragon above the entry area — outside the ride itself) is more intense atmospherically than the ride.
- **Motion sickness risk:** Moderate — screen sequences plus physical movement; not as intense as Forbidden Journey
- **Water:** Light effects; minor mist in some sequences
- **Drop:** Small drops as part of the coaster track; not a major drop element

## Express Pass
- **Yes — Express Pass covers Gringotts**
- Without Express: waits can hit 60–90 min on busy days, especially after the park opens fully

## Strategy notes
- **Morning priority in Diagon Alley** — use early admission to reach Gringotts before standby builds. This is a consistent top-3 ride at USF.
- **The queue is worth experiencing:** The Gringotts lobby pre-show and the goblin animatronics inside the bank are part of the attraction. Don't skip the interactive queue elements.
- **Adjacency:** Diagon Alley is a self-contained area. Once inside: Ollivanders Diagon Alley (walk-in wand selection show), Leaky Cauldron (best QS in USF), Wiseacre's for wand interactions throughout the land.
- **Combine with Hogwarts Express** on Park-to-Park days: Diagon Alley (USF) → King's Cross → Hogwarts Express → Hogsmeade (IOA) is the canonical Harry Potter experience. Only possible with Park-to-Park ticket.

## Trip relevance
High — one of the two best rides at USF (alongside Revenge of the Mummy). Essential for Harry Potter fans.

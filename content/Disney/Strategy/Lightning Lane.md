# Disney Lightning Lane Strategy

## System overview (2025–2026)

Disney currently sells three skip-the-line products. You do not need to buy all of them.

| Product | What it covers | Cost range |
|---|---|---|
| **Lightning Lane Multi Pass (LLMP)** | Most attractions — tiered system, pre-book up to 3 | $15–$45/person/day (dynamic) |
| **Lightning Lane Single Pass (LLSP)** | Only the 5 highest-demand rides; 1 per purchase | $11–$25/person/ride (dynamic) |
| **Lightning Lane Premier Pass** | Everything — all LLMP + all LLSP, unlimited | Up to $449/person/day (peak; generally not worth it except Easter week) |

**For this trip:** LLMP + selective LLSP purchases is the practical approach for most days.

---

## Multi Pass rules

- Book up to **3 attractions** per day at park opening (or 7 days ahead as Disney resort guests at Pop Century).
- At tiered parks (MK, EPCOT, DHS), you can only pre-book **1 Tier 1 attraction** — the rest must be Tier 2.
- After using a booking (or 2 hours pass), you can add a new one.
- **Animal Kingdom has no tiers** — pick any 3 from the Multi Pass pool.

## Single Pass rules

- Maximum **2 Single Passes** per person per day, across all parks.
- Same 7-day/3-day advance window as Multi Pass (resort guests / off-site).
- Can be bought without Multi Pass.
- **Sell out fast** on busy days — book at 7 AM (or earlier on peak dates) if you want them.

---

## Single Pass rides — the only 5

These rides are **not in Multi Pass**. You buy a separate Single Pass per ride.

| Park | Ride | Approx. price range | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Magic Kingdom | TRON Lightcycle / Run | $19–$23 | Usually the busiest MK ride |
| Magic Kingdom | Seven Dwarfs Mine Train | $11–$15 | Lower price reflects slower demand than TRON |
| EPCOT | Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind | $16–$22 | Frequently sells out |
| Hollywood Studios | Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance | $20–$25 | Most expensive LLSP at WDW |
| Animal Kingdom | Avatar: Flight of Passage | $15–$19 | Often sells out by 9 AM on busy days |

All prices are dynamic and will be higher during Easter week and other peak dates.
Prices sourced from 2025–early 2026 reports; verify in My Disney Experience closer to trip.

## Booking timing for Pop Century guests (resort benefit)

- You can book LLMP + LLSP **7 days before each park day**.
- Set a reminder for 6:45–7:00 AM Eastern on the booking day.
- Guardians, Rise of the Resistance, and Flight of Passage LLSP can sell out in minutes on busy days.
- Mid-to-late April (your dates) is moderate crowd period — booking day-of is risky but sometimes possible for Tier 2. Don't risk it for LLSP.

## General strategy tips

- Never use LLMP for a ride with a short standby. Save slots for high-wait rides.
- Once you use a LLMP booking, immediately book your next one.
- PeopleMover and Carousel of Progress have no LL — good filler while burning standby waits elsewhere.
- Use the **My Disney Experience app** to monitor standby times in real time and book LLMP refreshes.
- On Animal Kingdom day: book Flight of Passage LLSP at 7 AM, then build the morning around early standby for Kilimanjaro Safaris (which opens at park opening).

# Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind

## Type
- Coaster (reverse-launch indoor coaster with rotating vehicles; "omnicoaster")

## Location
- **Neighborhood:** World Discovery, EPCOT
- Back-right of the park; inside a large pavilion (the "Wonders of Xandar" building)

## Community sentiment score
- **9.8/10** — Sentiment tier: **Elite**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- Indoor roller coaster in a blacked-out building where vehicles rotate to face the screen and action at key moments. Launches backwards first, then forward. Soundtrack changes periodically (multiple songs in rotation — guests don't always know which they'll get). One of the longest indoor coasters at Walt Disney World.
- Ride duration: ~3 min

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 42" (107 cm)
- **Intensity:** High — the backwards launch and vehicle rotation create strong spatial disorientation. Faster and more intense than most Disney attractions.
- **Motion sickness risk:** High for guests sensitive to motion. The rotation combined with a dark enclosed space makes this one of the most likely Disney rides to affect sensitive guests.
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** No significant drops; the intensity is from speed and rotation

## Lightning Lane
- **Single Pass only** — not included in Multi Pass
- Approx. price: $16–$22/person (dynamic)
- Frequently sells out; book at 7 AM (7 days ahead as Pop Century guest)
- Do not attempt standby on a busy day — can reach 60–90+ min within the first hour

## Strategy notes
- **LLSP is the right call** for almost all visits. The experience is worth the cost.
- **If you want to ride multiple songs/soundtracks:** The rotating soundtrack is a major draw for fans; some guests ride twice. If this interests you, consider buying LLSP and also checking standby late in the evening.
- **Adjacency:** Mission: SPACE and (when operating) Test Track are close in World Discovery. If Test Track is open: good cluster for one section of the morning.

## Trip relevance
High — frequently ranked as the best or among the best rides at EPCOT. Essential for coaster fans.

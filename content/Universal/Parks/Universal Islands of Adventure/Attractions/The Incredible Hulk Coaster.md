# The Incredible Hulk Coaster

<img src="../../../../../Assets/Attraction Images/Islands of Adventure/hulk-coaster.jpg" alt="The Incredible Hulk Coaster" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;" />


## Type
- Coaster (launched steel coaster; multiple inversions)

## Location
- **Land:** Marvel Super Hero Island, Universal Islands of Adventure
- Immediately visible and audible from the park entrance; the launch sound is distinctive

## Community sentiment score
- **8.8/10** — Sentiment tier: **Strong**
- Basis: public-source consensus reviewed Mar 2026. Planning guidance, not an official rating.

## What it is
- The classic launch coaster at the IOA entrance. Multiple inversions, loud launch (magnetic catapult to 40 mph in 2 seconds), strong forces throughout. One of the most reliable coasters in Orlando for pure thrill delivery.
- Ride duration: ~2.5 min

## Physical characteristics
- **Height requirement:** 54" (137 cm) — one of the highest requirements in Orlando; verify before assuming both guests can ride
- **Intensity:** Very high — multiple full inversions (7 total), strong launch, rough by modern standards but iconic. More intense than VelociCoaster in terms of traditional coaster feel.
- **Motion sickness risk:** High — full inversions; not suitable for anyone with inversion sensitivity
- **Water:** No
- **Drop:** Multiple and as part of inversions; the first inversion immediately follows the launch

## Express Pass
- **Yes — Express Pass covers The Incredible Hulk Coaster**
- Standby typically 30–60 min at mid-morning; one of the more manageable IOA waits compared to Hagrid's or VelociCoaster

## Strategy notes
- **Good secondary coaster at IOA** — after Hagrid's and VelociCoaster, Hulk is the natural next thrill priority.
- Location at the front of the park (near the IOA entrance) makes it accessible as a first stop for guests not targeting Hogsmeade or Jurassic World first.
- **Adjacency:** Marvel Super Hero Island includes Doctor Doom's Fearfall (drop tower) and The Amazing Adventures of Spider-Man (dark ride). Good cluster for the Marvel area.
- The coaster's height requirement (54") is the highest at IOA — worth double-checking before planning around it.

## Trip relevance
High for coaster fans — delivers strong thrills; lower overall priority than Hagrid's and VelociCoaster but a clear strong secondary.

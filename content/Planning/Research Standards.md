# Research Standards

## What belongs here
- Evergreen park structure
- Attraction lists and park identities
- Early entry / early admission strategy
- Resort transportation and planning implications
- Notes that help later itinerary building

## What must stay marked as variable
- Exact operating hours
- Exact early-entry attraction lineup
- Virtual queue / Lightning Lane / Express details if they change
- Dining availability
- Seasonal entertainment
- Refurbishments and closures

## Source posture
Prefer official Disney and Universal sources for park/resort basics. If an item is likely to change, label it as **verify closer to trip**.

## Planning convention
For each park, capture:
1. Why the park matters
2. Best use of early entry
3. Anchor attractions
4. Secondary attractions
5. Shows / nighttime value
6. Friction points
7. Open questions for Rodrigo + Mariangeles

## Community-sentiment summary
- Strongest public-love cluster: **Gringotts, Mummy, Diagon Alley, Bourne, Horror Make-Up Show, E.T., MEN IN BLACK**
- Most mixed cluster: **Jimmy Fallon, Simpsons Ride, Villain-Con, Despicable Me** depending on simulator tolerance
- Most commonly weak/skippable sentiment: **Fast & Furious – Supercharged**
- Kid/family skew cluster: DreamWorks Land play areas and family entertainment

## Strategy sketch
- Morning: either commit hard to Diagon Alley / Gringotts or clear Mummy + another headliner first
- Midday: use Bourne, Horror Make-Up Show, and indoor attractions to absorb heat
- Afternoon / evening: hop if needed, especially if Islands has stronger late value

## Friction points
- Simulators can stack quickly and can be fatiguing if overdone
- Harry Potter zones tempt slower pacing early, which can cost ride efficiency
- A hop plan only works if morning priorities are already secured

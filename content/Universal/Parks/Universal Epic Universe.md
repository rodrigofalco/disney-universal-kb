## Park role
Newest and most strategically volatile park in the trip. Potentially the biggest swing day of the whole Universal segment.

## Best use case on this trip
Treat this as a highly focused day built around your top land priorities, not a casual roam-and-decide park.

## Current trip fit
- Strong current candidate for **Fri 17 Apr 2026**.
- This placement works well as the third Universal ticket day after a lighter **16 Apr** flex/shopping day.
- If this remains the plan, Epic should be treated as one of the most tightly managed mornings of the Universal segment.

## Early-admission posture
See [[../Strategy/Early Park Admission]]. If Epic is included for your dates, it may be the highest-leverage early start of the entire Universal stay.

- Strongest public-love cluster: **Battle at the Ministry, Stardust Racers, Monsters Unchained**

## Strategy sketch
- Pick your top land before arrival; do not decide at the portal
- Build the first hour around the single hardest-to-recover attraction
- Keep the day geographically disciplined; repeated cross-park moves will hurt
- Expect this park to need the most rule-checking closer to trip because it is newer

## Friction points
- New-park operations can change quickly
- Demand may cluster by land, not just by single ride
- Social-media hype can distort what is actually worth prioritizing for you

## Open questions
- Which land matters most emotionally?
- Is this primarily a thrill day, an immersion day, or a balanced sampler?
- Should this be your earliest and most tightly managed Universal morning?
